Terms & Privacy

Purpose: This tool is for team communication and self-reflection only. It is not used for hiring, promotion, or compensation decisions.

Data: We store your responses locally to show you your result and to provide an aggregate team view. The name must be unique; pseudonyms are allowed.

Access: The team facilitator can access aggregate counts via an admin page. Exports (CSV) are also limited to the facilitator.

Deletion: After submission, you receive a one-time delete link (token). Use it to remove your data at any time. You can also contact the facilitator to request deletion.

Transparency: Scores are indicative only. Results should be used as a starting point for discussion, not as labels.
