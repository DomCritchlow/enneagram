The Nine Enneagram Types

The Enneagram reveals nine distinct patterns of thinking, feeling, and acting. Each type represents a unique worldview shaped by core motivations, fears, and desires. Understanding all types enhances empathy, communication, and team collaboration.

Remember: Everyone has access to all types, but one will resonate as your "home base" - your dominant pattern of motivation and behavior.
